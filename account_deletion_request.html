Kobciye — Account Deletion Request

Last Updated: January 2025

⚠️ Important: Account Deletion is Permanent

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your login credentials and profile information
  • All your personal data associated with the account
  • Access to all business data and features

This action cannot be undone. Please ensure you have exported any data you wish to keep before requesting account deletion.

How to Request Account Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your Kobciye account and all associated data. This page explains how to make such a request and what will happen to your data.

Steps to Request Account Deletion:

  1. Export Your Data (Optional but Recommended): Before deleting your account, you may want to export your business data, reports, or other information you wish to keep.
  2. Contact Us: Send an email to mohamedbadhey@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Provide Information: Include the following in your email:
    • Your username or email address associated with your Kobciye account
    • Confirmation that you want to delete your account permanently
    • Any specific reason (optional, helps us improve our service)
  4. Verification: We will verify your identity to ensure the account belongs to you before processing the deletion
  5. Processing: We will process your account deletion request within 30 days
  6. Confirmation: You will receive confirmation once your account has been deleted

What Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password, profile settings
  • Personal Data: Name, phone number, address, preferences
  • User-Generated Content: Product images, branding assets you uploaded
  • Access Rights: All access to the Kobciye app and your business data
  • Authentication Tokens: All login sessions and tokens

What May Be Retained

Important: The following data may be retained for legal, accounting, or business continuity purposes:

  • Business Transaction Data: Sales records, invoices, and financial transactions (required for tax and accounting compliance - typically 7 years)
  • Inventory Records: Historical inventory data (required for business records)
  • System Logs: Audit logs of system activities (required for security and compliance - typically 1-2 years)
  • Legal Requirements: Data required to be retained by law or accounting regulations
  • Backup Data: Data in backups may be retained for up to 90 days before permanent deletion

Note: If you are a business owner and your business has other users, the business data will remain accessible to other authorized users. Only your personal account and access will be deleted.

Data Retention Period

  • Account Data: Deleted immediately upon account deletion (unless retention required by law)
  • Personal Information: Removed from active systems within 30 days
  • Business Transaction Data: Retained as required by law or accounting regulations (typically 7 years for tax purposes)
  • System Logs: Retained for security and compliance purposes (typically 1-2 years)
  • Backup Data: May be retained in backups for up to 90 days before permanent deletion

Alternative: Delete Specific Data Only

If you want to keep your account but delete specific data, you can request partial data deletion instead. Visit our Data Deletion Request page for more information.

Response Time

We will:

  • Acknowledge your request within 7 business days
  • Verify your identity (to protect your account security)
  • Process the account deletion within 30 days
  • Confirm completion via email

If additional verification is required or there are legal requirements preventing immediate deletion, we will notify you of any delays.
