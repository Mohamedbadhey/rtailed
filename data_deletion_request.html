Kobciye — Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: January 2025

How to Request Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from Kobciye without deleting your entire account. This page explains how to make such a request and what data will be deleted or retained.

Steps to Request Data Deletion:

  1. Contact Us: Send an email to mohamedbadhey@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Provide Information: Include your username or email address associated with your Kobciye account
  3. Specify Data: Clearly indicate which data you want deleted (see options below)
  4. Verification: We will verify your identity before processing the request
  5. Processing: We will process your request within 30 days

What Data Can Be Deleted

You can request deletion of the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, address
  • Profile Data: Username, profile settings, preferences
  • Customer Data: Customer names, contact information (if you manage customers)
  • Product Images: Product photos you have uploaded
  • Branding Assets: Business logos and branding images you have uploaded

What Data Will Be Retained

Important: The following data may be retained for legal, accounting, or business continuity purposes:

  • Business Transaction Data: Sales records, invoices, and financial transactions (required for tax and accounting compliance)
  • Inventory Records: Historical inventory data (required for business records)
  • System Logs: Audit logs of system activities (required for security and compliance)
  • Legal Requirements: Data required to be retained by law or accounting regulations

Data Retention Period

  • Account Data: Deleted immediately upon request (unless retention required by law)
  • Business Transaction Data: Retained as required by law or accounting regulations (typically 7 years for tax purposes)
  • System Logs: Retained for security and compliance purposes (typically 1-2 years)
  • Backup Data: May be retained in backups for up to 90 days before permanent deletion

Account Deletion vs. Data Deletion

You have two options:

  • Partial Data Deletion: Request deletion of specific data types while keeping your account active
  • Complete Account Deletion: Delete your entire account and all associated data (except data required by law)

You can request either option by contacting us using the information below.

Response Time

We will acknowledge your request within 7 business days and complete the deletion process within 30 days, unless:

  • Legal requirements prevent immediate deletion
  • Additional verification is required
  • Complex requests require additional time (we will notify you)
