Kobciye Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Kobciye ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Kobciye mobile application (the "App"). This policy applies to all users of our retail management system.

Important: This application requires an active internet connection to function properly. All core features, including data synchronization, authentication, and API communication, depend on network connectivity. The app connects to our hosted backend API to provide real-time retail management services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our App.

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, password, email address, and business details (business name, address, contact information)
  • Business Data: Store information, product details, inventory data, sales records, pricing information
  • Customer Data: Customer names, contact information, purchase history (as entered by you for your business records)
  • Transaction Data: Sales transactions, payment information, receipts, invoices
  • User Content: Product images, business branding (logos, colors), custom categories, notes, and other content you create
  • Profile Information: User profile settings, preferences, language settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature usage, session duration, pages viewed, actions taken within the app
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, network information, crash reports, error logs
  • Location Data: General location for multi-store management (only if you enable location services and only for business management purposes)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise GPS location data unless you explicitly enable it
  • We do not collect biometric data (fingerprints, face recognition)
  • We do not collect sensitive financial information beyond what is necessary for transaction processing
  • We do not collect information from third-party social media accounts unless you explicitly connect them

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the Kobciye service
  • Process transactions and manage inventory
  • Generate reports and analytics for your business
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important updates and notifications about the service
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Server Storage: Your business data is stored securely on our servers to enable synchronization and backup
  • Local Storage: Some data may be cached locally on your device for offline access
  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit (using HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (using encryption algorithms)
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms ensure only authorized users can access your data
  • Regular Security Updates: We regularly update our security measures and monitor for potential vulnerabilities
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Network Security: All API communications use secure protocols

While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is your business data, and we respect your privacy. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in app functionality (such as cloud hosting services). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets (with prior notice to users)
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of users, our service, or others

We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing companies.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data and business information stored in our systems
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information through the app settings or by contacting us
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account through the app settings, which will remove your account and associated data from our servers
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a readable format (CSV, PDF, or other formats) through the app's export features
  • Opt-out: Opt-out of non-essential communications by adjusting notification settings in the app
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us directly
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time (note: this may affect app functionality)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 13 (Contact Us). We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Transaction data: As required by tax and business regulations
  • Usage data: Typically 2 years for analytics purposes
  • Customer data: As long as you maintain it in the system

8. Children's Privacy

Kobciye is intended for business use and is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party services or integrate with third-party APIs for essential functionality. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.

Third-Party Services We May Use:

  • Cloud Hosting: We use cloud hosting services to store and process your data securely
  • Analytics: We may use analytics services to understand app usage and improve functionality (no personally identifiable information is shared)
  • Payment Processing: If payment features are used, payment processing is handled by secure third-party payment processors

We only work with third-party service providers who have strong privacy and security practices and are contractually obligated to protect your data.

11. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending an email notification (if you have provided an email address)
  • Displaying a prominent notice in the app

Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - for users in the European Union
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - for users in California, USA
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) - we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • Other applicable data protection laws in jurisdictions where we operate

We are committed to protecting your privacy rights regardless of your location.

13. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our app may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Analyze app usage patterns

You can control cookie preferences through your device settings, though disabling cookies may affect app functionality.
