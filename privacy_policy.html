Kobciye Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Kobciye (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Kobciye mobile application (“App”).

Important: Kobciye requires an active internet connection because all features such as login, inventory, products, sales, and data syncing communicate with our backend server.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information (username, password, email, business details)
  • Business Data (products, inventory, pricing, categories, reports)
  • Customer Data (names, phone numbers, purchase history)
  • Transaction Data (sales, payments, invoices)
  • User Content (product images, logos)
  • App preferences (language, settings)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Type, Operating System, App Version
  • IP Address (used only for secure server communication)
  • Network Status (to verify connection)
  • Basic usage logs processed locally (no analytics SDK)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • GPS location
  • Biometric data
  • IMEI or hardware identifiers
  • Analytics or advertising data
  • Crash logs (no crash SDK installed)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the Kobciye service
  • Manage products, sales, and inventory
  • Securely store business and transaction data
  • Generate reports (PDF exports)
  • Authenticate accounts
  • Improve performance and stability
  • Enable secure backend communication

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Server: Business data stored securely on our backend server.
  • Local: PDFs, settings, and tokens stored only on your device.
  • Encryption: All communication uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • Passwords: Hash-protected, never stored in plain text.
  • Tokens: Saved using secure encrypted storage.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do NOT sell or share your data with advertisers or analytics providers.

We may share data only in these limited cases:

  • With your consent
  • With secure hosting/service providers
  • For legal requirements
  • During business transfer (with notice)

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion
  • Export your business data
  • Withdraw consent (may affect functionality)

7. Account Deletion

You may delete your account through the app or by contacting support.

When deleted:

  • Credentials and profile data are removed permanently
  • Business data may be kept only if legally required

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: kept until account deletion
  • Business & transaction data: kept according to law
  • PDF files: stored only on your device

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is for business use and not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be stored on servers outside your country with proper safeguards.

11. Third-Party Services

Kobciye does not use analytics, ads, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

We only use:

  • Secure cloud hosting for backend storage
  • Google Fonts CDN (no personal data collected)

12. Permissions Used

  • INTERNET – required for syncing data with the server
  • CAMERA (optional) – for taking product photos
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (optional) – select photos from gallery
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – show local notifications (Android 13+)

13. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will appear in the app and on our website.
