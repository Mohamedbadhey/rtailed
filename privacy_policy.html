Kobciye Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Kobciye ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Kobciye mobile application ("App").

Important: This app requires an active internet connection. All core functionalities (login, product management, sales, inventory updates, and data synchronization) rely on communication with our backend server.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, password, email address, business details.
  • Business Data: Products, inventory, pricing, categories, reports.
  • Customer Data: Customer names, phone numbers, purchase history.
  • Transaction & Financial Data: Sales transactions, payment methods, invoice details.
  • User Content: Product images, business logo, branding information.
  • Profile Settings: Language preferences, app configuration.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, app version, operating system.
  • Technical Data: IP address (for secure server communication), network status.
  • Usage Data: Basic app interactions (screens accessed, actions performed) — processed locally and not sent to analytics services.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise GPS location
  • Biometric identifiers
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID)
  • Crash logs (no crash reporting SDK installed)
  • Analytics data (no analytics SDK)
  • Advertising or tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the Kobciye service
  • Process sales and manage inventory
  • Store business, customer, and transaction data
  • Generate and export reports (PDFs)
  • Authenticate user accounts
  • Improve performance and fix issues
  • Secure communication with our backend server

4. Data Storage and Security

We apply industry-standard security protections:

  • Server Storage: Business data stored on our secure backend server.
  • Local Storage: PDF reports, settings, and authentication tokens stored on the device.
  • Encryption: All communications use HTTPS/TLS.
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Token Security: Authentication tokens are stored using encrypted secure storage.

While no method of digital transmission is 100% secure, we actively maintain strong protections and monitor for vulnerabilities.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell or share your data with advertisers or analytics providers. We may share data only in the following limited cases:

  • With Your Consent — if you request or authorize sharing.
  • Service Providers — cloud hosting providers who help operate our backend (they are contractually obligated to protect data).
  • Legal Requirements — if required by law or court order.
  • Business Transfer — in case of merger or acquisition (you will be notified).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your business data
  • Withdraw consent (this may limit app functionality)

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Account Deletion

Users may delete their account at any time through the app or by contacting support. When an account is deleted:

  • Login credentials and profile data are permanently removed.
  • Business data may be retained only if required for legal or tax purposes.

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until account deletion.
  • Business & transaction data: Retained as required by law or accounting regulations.
  • Local PDF files: Stored only on your device.

9. Children’s Privacy

Kobciye is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country. Appropriate safeguards are applied to protect your data.

11. Third-Party Services

The app does NOT use analytics, ads, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

We only use:

  • Cloud Hosting: secure backend server for storing and processing business data.
  • Google Fonts CDN: for font loading (no personal data collected).

12. Permissions Used

  • INTERNET: Required for syncing business data with the backend.
  • CAMERA (optional): Capture product images.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (optional): Select images from gallery.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): Display local notifications.

13. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on our website. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
