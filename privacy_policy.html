Kobciye Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Kobciye ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Kobciye mobile application ("App").

Important: This app requires an active internet connection. All core functionalities (login, product management, sales, inventory updates, and data synchronization) rely on communication with our backend server.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, password, email address, business details.
  • Business Data: Products, inventory, pricing, categories, reports.
  • Customer Data: Customer names, phone numbers, purchase history.
  • Transaction Data: Sales transactions, payment methods.
  • User Content: Product images, business logo, branding information.
  • Profile Settings: Language preferences, app configuration.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, app version, operating system.
  • Technical Data: IP address (for secure server communication), network status.
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage information (buttons clicked, screens accessed). This data is processed locally and not sent to analytics services.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise GPS location
  • Biometric identifiers
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID)
  • Crash logs (no crash reporting SDK)
  • Analytics data (no analytics SDK installed)
  • Advertising or tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the Kobciye service
  • Process sales, manage inventory, and store business data
  • Generate and export reports
  • Allow login authentication and secure access
  • Improve app performance and fix issues
  • Secure communication with our backend server

4. Data Storage and Security

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

  • Server Storage: Business data is stored on our secure backend server.
  • Local Storage: Some data (such as PDF reports, settings, and secure tokens) is stored locally.
  • Encryption: All communication uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Token Security: Authentication tokens are stored using secure, encrypted storage.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we actively maintain strong security protections.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell or share your personal information with third-party advertisers or analytics companies.

We may share data only in the following cases:

  • With Your Consent: If you request or authorize sharing.
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting providers who help operate our backend (they cannot use your data for other purposes).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or government order.
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger or acquisition (you will be notified).

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your business data
  • Withdraw consent (may limit app functionality)

You may exercise these rights by contacting us at any time.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained until you delete your account.
  • Business & transaction data: retained as required for legal and accounting compliance.
  • Local files (PDFs): stored only on your device.

8. Children's Privacy

Kobciye is designed for business use only. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

10. Third-Party Services

The Kobciye app does NOT use analytics, ads, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

We only use:

  • Cloud Hosting: Secure backend server for storing business data.
  • Google Fonts CDN: For displaying fonts (no data is collected).

11. Permissions Used

  • INTERNET: Required for syncing business data with the backend.
  • CAMERA (optional): Capture product images.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (optional): Select images from device gallery.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): For local notifications.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on our website. Your continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
